DINING

Hungry for some good sweets or fine cuisine? Well check out some our amazing restaurants, all of which serve a varity of good food for anyone to enjoy!!

Sugar Cube Corner

Owned by the lovely Cake family, Sugarcube Corner is known for bring the number one place for ponies and creatures alike to get different pastries and desserts. Ranging from cupcakes, birthday cakes, muffins, doughnuts, many more!!

The Tasty Treat

Located in the Restaurant Row with some of Canterlot's well reviewed eating establishments. The Tasty Treat serves some great dishes from Indian culture, it used to be very underrated until Pinkie Pie and Rarity would help the owners gain more attention and beat out the so called critics.
